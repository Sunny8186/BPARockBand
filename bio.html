MEET THE BAND

Jaxon Sharp, Guitarist

I was born on May 22, 2005, in Toledo, Ohio. My parents were very poor, and were eager to save up every cent they had in order to raise me well. My friends, Luna and Ray, told me to start a band with them, and I agreed. I knew it would be my only chance to become rich and famous.

Luna Welkes, Bassist

I was born on June 2, 2004, in Sylvania, Ohio. I moved to Toledo when I was 5, where I met Jaxon and Ray. It was my idea that we should make up a rock band together, because we were friends for so long. This goes to show that frienship can go a long way past childhood.

Ray Wilkinson, Drummer

I was born on December 21, 2004, in Toledo, Ohio. I am the son of the famous singer Billy Wilkinson, and it was he who persuaded me to start a musical career. I released two albums as a solo artist, My Journey (2021) and Sunshine (2023), before starting the band with my friends. I actually chose the name Stage Fright because it emphasized that we all had to face our fears before we go big.
