MEET THE BAND

Jaxon Sharp- Guitarist, Vocalist

Jaxon Sharp is a Toledo native who grew up poor in money but rich in love and music. He used his talents to work his way through school and learn guitar to compliment his singing voice so that he could make his way to where his is today, rocking out with his best friends Luna and Ray.

Luna Welkes- Bassist

Luna moved to Toledo in 2009 where she became fast friends with Jaxon and Ray and learned to play the bass guitar. When asked if she wanted to join a small garage band she never expected to go this far.

Ray Wilkinson- Drummer

Ray released two albums as a solo artist, My Journey (2021) and Sunshine (2023), before starting the band with his friends. ray actually chose the name Stage Fright because it emphasized the fears that the group faced about performing in front of an audience.
